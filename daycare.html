Daycare Description

At Esther’s Kid’s Corner, we believe every child deserves a safe, loving, and stimulating environment where they can learn, grow, and play at their own pace. Nestled in a quiet residential neighborhood, our cozy in-home daycare offers a structured yet flexible routine tailored to meet the developmental needs of infants, toddlers, and preschoolers.

Our days are filled with fun learning experiences like storytime, creative art projects, outdoor play, music, and basic early learning activities that build social, emotional, and cognitive skills. We also provide nutritious home-cooked meals and snacks to keep little tummies full and happy.

With over 8 years of childcare experience and certifications in First Aid and CPR, we’re committed to giving your child personalized attention in a small group setting. We maintain a warm, family-like atmosphere where children feel comfortable and parents feel confident..

Whether you're looking for full-time care or a part-time arrangement, we’d love to welcome your little one into our daycare family.
